# HOMESCHOOLING SUPPORT - COMPREHENSIVE OVERVIEW

## How Homeschooling is Addressed in LinguaAccess

### Executive Summary

Homeschooling represents a critical market segment for LinguaAccess, particularly in India where families of children with learning disabilities often choose homeschooling due to inadequate support in traditional schools. The platform addresses homeschooling through:

1. **Dual-Role Parent Accounts** - Parents function as both educator and parent
2. **Teaching Guidance System** - Step-by-step instructional support for non-trained educators
3. **Flexible Curriculum Planning** - Self-paced, customizable learning paths
4. **Multi-Child Family Management** - Single account manages multiple learners
5. **Homeschool Co-op Integration** - Virtual community spaces for collaborative learning
6. **Portfolio & Documentation** - Regulatory compliance (NIOS/state requirements)
7. **Affordable Family Pricing** - Recognition of single-family budget constraints
8. **Parent Training Resources** - Professional development for teaching language to learners with disabilities
